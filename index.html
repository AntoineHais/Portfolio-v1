Projects

Ag'kathon


What is this?

This is a hackathon focused on the agri-agro sector! The principle: the participants, organised in small multidisciplinary teams of students, graduates or company employees, must produce a form of innovation to respond to a given problem... all in 36 hours. A jury of professionals will then present the proposed project, followed by the awarding of prizes after deliberation.


Constraint

Design of a video game for primary school children. The game should make the link between fruit and vegetables and the carbon footprint of imports.


Production

The game features 4 players who go to a super market to pick up seasonal fruit and vegetables and bring them to their shopping carts. If the player does not bring seasonal fruit or vegetables, their carbon score will increase once the 4 seasons have passed and the player with the lowest carbon score wins.
The seasons in the game are symbolized by 4 events that are present in each season :
  • Easter for spring
  • Beach for the summer
  • Halloween for Autumn
  • Christmas for winter


Skills & Knowledge

  • Working with Unity and C#
  • Efficient teamwork with complete strangers
  • Presenting a project to an audience


Result

At the end of the Ag'Kathon, the project we had realised was placed 3rd out of the 10 participating teams.
